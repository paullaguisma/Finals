THE BRANDED AND APOSTLES

"Even if we painstakingly piece together something lost, it doesn’t mean things will ever go back to how they were." - Guts

GUTS

The protagonist of the series, known as the "Black Swordsman." A fierce warrior wielding the massive Dragonslayer sword, Guts embodies resilience and the will to fight against impossible odds despite his tragic past.

GRIFFITH

The ambitious and charismatic leader of the Band of the Hawk. Griffith dreams of ruling his own kingdom, but his descent into darkness leads to betrayal and his transformation into Femto, a member of the God Hand.

CASCA

A skilled warrior and second-in-command of the Band of the Hawk. She shares a complicated relationship with both Guts and Griffith. Her life takes a tragic turn after the Eclipse.

SKULL KNIGHT

A mysterious, undead knight who aids Guts and warns him of impending danger. He appears to have a deep history with the God Hand and the world of causality.

PUCK

A cheerful and mischievous elf who becomes one of Guts' earliest companions. Puck provides moments of levity and emotional support amid the dark themes of the series.

ZODD

A powerful, centuries-old apostle who relishes battle. Initially an adversary of Guts, Zodd comes to respect his strength and courage, appearing throughout the series as both foe and unlikely ally.

About me

Hi, I'm Paul Laguisma, a passionate fan of *Berserk* and all things gritty and inspiring. When I’m not delving into Kentaro Miura’s dark and intricate world, you’ll find me boxing—channeling my inner Guts as I face challenges head-on—or reading mangas, seeking stories that ignite my imagination. Just like Guts wields his sword with unrelenting determination, I aspire to forge my path as a computer engineer, building solutions that stand resilient in a constantly evolving world. My journey, much like *Berserk*, is about perseverance, growth, and striving for greatness in the face of adversity. Welcome to my website, where passion and purpose collide!
